综合写作

  1. The lecture is in conflict with the reading passage about __. The lecturer believes that __.

  2. First, the reading passage suggests that ___. However, the lecture is totally opposed. The lecturer stresses that __.

  3. Second, the lecturer rejects the point in the reading passage that ___. The reading claims that ___, while the lecturer argues that ___.(However, the lecturer argues that ___.)

  4. Third, the lecturer disagrees with the final point in the reading passage that ___. The lecturer insists that ___.

  • objects to
